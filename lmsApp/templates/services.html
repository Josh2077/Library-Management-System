WHAT IS ONLINE LIBRARY MANAGEMENT SYSTEM ?
Library plays an important role in all schools and colleges, no educational institution can exist without Library Administration Software. It is an important part of every school and college, and it helps the librarian to keep records of available books as well as issued books. Online Library Management System helps in different ways by providing students the facility to learn, gather resources, promote group learning and improve knowledge and skills.
